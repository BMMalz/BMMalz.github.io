Academic Calendar

Fall 2017

Monday, August 28Orientation for all new students.

Tuesday, August 29 Registration for all new students.

Wednesday, August 30, 8:20 A.M. Fall-term classes begin.

Monday, September 4Labor day - classes do not meet - offices closed.

Monday, September 18Last day to add a course with permission of registrar.

Tuesday, October 17, 11:00 P.M. October recess begins.

Monday, October 23, 8:20 A.M. October recess ends; Classes resume.

Friday, October 27 Last day to drop a course with permission of registrar.

Friday, October 27, 12:00 P.M. Midterm grades due.

Thursday, November 16 Annual All-School Open House.

Friday, November 17, 5:30 P.M. November recess begins.

Monday, November 27, 8:20 A.M. Classes resume.

Friday, December 8 Last day to withdraw from course with permission of instructor and registrar.

Friday, December 8, 5:30 P.M.Fall-term classes end.

Friday, December 8 Reading period begins.

Thursday, December 14, 9:00 A.M. Critiques and examinations begin.

Wednesday, December 20, 5:30 P.M. Critiques and examinations end.

Wednesday, December 20 Winter recess begins.

Spring 2018

Wednesday, January 3, 12 A.M. EST Closing date for submission of online applications for admission in September 2017.

Monday, January 15 Martin Luther King Day; classes do not meet, offices closed.

Tuesday, January 16 Registration for all students.

Tuesday, January 16 Academic classes begin.

Wednesday, January 17, 8:20 A.M. Spring-term School of Art classes begin.

Monday, February 5 Last day to add a course with permission of registrar.

Friday, March 9 Last day to drop a course with permission of registrar.

Friday, March 9, 12:00 P.M.Midterm grades due.

Friday, March 9, 5:30 P.M. Spring recess begins.

Monday, March 26, 8:20 A.M. Spring recess ends; classes resume.

Friday, April 27 Last day to withdraw from a course with permission of instructor and registrar.

Friday, April 27, 5:30 P.M.Spring-term classes end.

Friday, April 27 Reading period begins.

Thursday, May 3, 9:00 A.M.Critiques and examinations begin.

Wednesday, May 9, 5:30 P.M.Critiques and examinations end.

Wednesday, May 9 Summer recess begins.

Monday, May 21University Commencement.
